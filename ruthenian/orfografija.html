Ruthenian Orthography

V naczalje sotvoril Bog njebo i zjemlju. Zjemlje zze byla bjezvidna i pusta, i tjma nad bjezdnoju, i Duh bozzij nosilsja nad vodoju. I skazal Bog: da budjet svjet. I stal svjet. I uvidjel Bog svjet, czto on horosz, i otdjelil Bog svjet ot tjmy.

[1] At the beginning of a word, JE is JE, and E is E. But if it is in the middle of a word, JE can be written as E. This is because E in the middle of a word is relatively rare. You could even write E in the middle of a word as 'E, that is Apostrophe E. This does not apply if it is after a vowel. Examples: Denj, Beloje.

[2] When combinations of independent letters from cyrillic combine in their transliterated form into characters in Latin which are digraphs, they are to be seperated by the apostrophe. Examples: ЦЗ = C'Z , СЗ = S'Z , ЗЗ = Z'Z , ЙA = J'A
